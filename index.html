Hi! I'm an aspiring physicist and physics+math major at Washington College. I grew up in India in the cities of Mumbai (मुंबई) and Bengaluru (ಬೆಂಗಳೂರು). I'm highly interested in particle physics and hope to attend graduate school soon.

I am currently working on studying dark matter abundance with Dr. Digesh Raut at my home university. In the past, I have contributed to the US CMS program as an intern at Fermilab, and have worked with data from the LHC with Dr. Suyog Shrestha.

You can find details of my research under the 'Research' tab.

As of October 2022, Washington College is now an ATLAS institution! Subsequently, I am working with Dr. Suyog Shrestha and Jason Ikenaga to set up a new particle physics laboratory at Washington College.

I am due to graduate a year early in 2023. My education is currently sponsored by Lawrence and Wendy Culp via the GE Chairman's scholarship, and the Hodson Trust via the Hodson Trust Scholarship.
